Generation Process

  1. Fetch FSN classifications (filtered by type/zone)
  2. Aggregate warehouse & club house inventory by FSN
  3. Create FSN × ClubHouse recommendations
  4. Check deracking within window (configurable days)
  5. Calculate: max(MDQ, SS + DRR×DOH)
  6. Allocate warehouse inventory (one-shot proportional)
  7. Auto-create STNs (no manual upload)
